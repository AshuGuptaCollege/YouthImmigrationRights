Undocumented Youth: In Public

Main Rights to Know in Public

  1. You have the right to remain silent.
    “Am I free to go?”
  2. The government is not supposed to treat you different based on your religion.
    Just be yourself.
  3. You don’t have to answer questions, but when you do, make sure it is accurate.
    You do not have to answer questions about where you were born or where you are from, but do not give false information about your identity or status or claim that you are a U.S. citizen if you are not.
