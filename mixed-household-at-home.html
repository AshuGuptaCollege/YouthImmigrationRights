Mixed Status Households: At Home

FOUR THINGS TO DO

  1. Don’t let immigration into your home unless they have a warrant
    The only reason you should let immigration into your home is if they have a warrant signed by a judge.
  2. Stay silent but don’t lie if you speak
  3. Interpret for your family
    Your non-English speaking family has a right to be spoken to in their best language. You can interpret for your non-English speaking family members. Don’t talk about yourself if you are interpreting. If immigration has a signed warrant to talk to one of your family members, you can interpret for them without being required to answer any personal questions about yourself.
  4. Know you can’t be deported as a US citizen
    If you are a citizen of the United States, you cannot be deported - even if other people in your household don’t have status.
