Mixed Status Households: At Home

Main Rights to Know at Home

  1. You do not have to let an agent into your home
    The only reason you should let an ICE agent into your home is if the agent has a warrant signed by a judge or magistrate.
  2. You have a right to remain silent - but you cannot lie if you speak
  3. You can interpret for your family if they need you to
    Your non-English speaking family has a right to be spoken to / interacted with in their best language.
  4. You do not have to talk about yourself if you are interpreting
    If the ICE agent has a signed warrant to talk to one of your family members, you can interpret for them without being required to answer any personal questions about yourself.
