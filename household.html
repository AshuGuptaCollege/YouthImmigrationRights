Your Rights With A Household With Different Immigration Statuses

  1. Don’t open your door for immigration at your home unless they have a warrant signed by a judge (4th A.).
  2. Don’t fight or resist a search – say that you do not consent.
  3. You have a right to remain silent (5th Amendment) → “Am I free to go?”
  4. Some searches are always allowed, including:
    • At borders, airports, and seaports
    • Some searching of cars
    • Searching things within reach during arrest
    • Searching things in plain view
    • Searching evidence that might be immediately destroyed
    • During pursuit of a suspect—don’t flee
