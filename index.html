Learn Your Rights in 5 Minutes or Less

Main Rights

  1. You have a right to remain silent
  2. You have a right to keep your door closed to immigration agents at your home (a right to refuse search)
  3. You have a right to speak to a lawyer before signing anything
  4. You--usually--have a right to talk to a judge before being deported
  5. You have a right to a court interpreter in deportation proceedings.
  6. You have a right to contact your consulate.
