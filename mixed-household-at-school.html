Mixed Status Households: At School

Main Rights to Know at School

  1. You have a right to go to school.
  2. You do not have to answer questions about your immigration status or the immigration status of your family members.
    If school officials or immigration ask you about your family, you do not have to answer them.
  3. You do not have to consent to a search of your person without a warrant.
    Your school might, however, give ICE permission to search your locker. This is a policy choice made by your school.
