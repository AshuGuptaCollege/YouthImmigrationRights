• You do not have a right to avoid capture → so don’t run and don’t resist arrest
    • Remain silent
    • Ask, “Am I free to go?”
  • You do not have a right to lie or mislead → you can be silent, but don’t lie.
  • You do not have a right to visit friends or family detained by immigration officials without also being detained if you don’t have an immigration status.
  • You do not have a right to fight or resist a search (you have a right to refuse it) → do not fight the agent conducting a search; say that you do not consent.
